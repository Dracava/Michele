Van der Veen’s

Van der Veen’s The Dutch Store

What this is
Vander Veen’s The Dutch Store is an online webshop offering direct imports of Dutch goods to US customers. Their sortiment includes a large selection of Dutch food products and souvenirs from well-known Dutch brands. Vander Veen’s strives to bring Dutch items one misses to US households. The target audience for this redesign are Dutch expats living in the US who want to purchase fresh Dutch foods.
Why we designed this
Dutch expats living abroad need an easy and enjoyable shopping experience, which the current website fails to deliver.

First we did a persona analysis to empathize better with the target group. Then we ran an expert review on the existing website and found that: The website is no longer in connection with the target audience. The website is dated and lacks vital fuctions e.g. option to filter and sort products and feedback messges when user action has been exectued. The overflow of information i.e. content is dense and overwhelms the user. There is no clear indentity and branding. The lack of visual hierarchy making website highly unreadable and hard to understand. Personal connection and building of trust between client and user is mininal.

Our design challenge: How might we redesign Vander Veen’s e-commerce website so that it is easy-to-use and attractive for Dutch expats who want to experience Dutch culture while living abroad?
